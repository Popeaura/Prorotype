About Us

Company Profile

Ilorie Consult Limited is a dynamic and client-focused financial and management consulting firm based in Nairobi, Kenya. Specializing in serving Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) in Kenya and beyond, we are committed to providing tailored solutions that address the unique challenges and opportunities faced by businesses of this scale.

Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to helping your business thrive through strategic financial planning, management consulting, and business development services.

Our Services

Accounts & Bookkeeping

Comprehensive accounting and bookkeeping services to keep your financial records accurate and up-to-date.

Valuation

Expert business valuation services to determine the true worth of your enterprise for investment, sale, or strategic planning.

Audit & Research

Thorough audit services and market research to identify opportunities and ensure compliance.

Business Development

Strategic business development consulting to help your enterprise grow and expand into new markets.

Tax Consultancy

Expert tax planning and consultancy services to optimize your tax position and ensure compliance with regulations.

Management Consulting

Tailored management consulting services to improve operational efficiency and strategic decision-making.
